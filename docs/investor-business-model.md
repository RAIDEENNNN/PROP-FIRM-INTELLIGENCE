# Investor and Business Model

## One-line pitch

FundedScope is the prop firm intelligence layer: compare firms, monitor rules, track spreads, verify payouts and help traders choose better-funded account opportunities.

## Problem

Prop firm traders face fragmented information:

- Rules are difficult to compare.
- Firms change policies.
- Reviews can be noisy or fake.
- Payout proof is scattered.
- Spreads and trading conditions are not centralized.
- Affiliate content often lacks transparency.

## Solution

FundedScope combines:

- IMDb-style firm profiles
- Trustpilot-style reviews
- Bloomberg/TradingView-style dashboards
- Spread intelligence
- Rule-change alerts
- Trader-fit recommendations
- Monetized affiliate and subscription infrastructure

## Target users

1. Retail traders buying challenges
2. Funded traders managing multiple accounts
3. Futures/FX/crypto prop firm traders
4. Affiliates and trading educators
5. Prop firm review communities
6. Firms wanting verified listings and visibility

## Revenue streams

| Revenue stream | Priority | Description |
|---|---:|---|
| Affiliate commissions | 1 | Earn commission when traders sign up through tracked links |
| Premium subscriptions | 2 | Pro trader tools, alerts, dashboards and recommendations |
| Featured listings | 3 | Clearly disclosed sponsored/featured placements |
| Business/API access | 4 | Data access for affiliates, educators and communities |
| Reports and analytics | 5 | Market intelligence reports and firm/category analysis |
| Sponsorships | 6 | Newsletter, page and report sponsorships |
| Display ads | 7 | Later-stage monetization once traffic is meaningful |

## Competitive differentiation

| Existing alternatives | FundedScope difference |
|---|---|
| Basic comparison blogs | Dynamic profiles, scoring, alerts and dashboard |
| Affiliate-only sites | Transparent affiliate disclosure and editorial policy |
| Review platforms | Prop-specific rules, payouts, spreads and challenge metrics |
| Spreads tools | Firm-aware trader intelligence layer |
| Manual spreadsheets | Searchable, filterable, account-based platform |

## MVP success metrics

- 1,000 monthly organic visitors
- 250 registered users
- 50 saved watchlists
- 25 review submissions
- 10 alert-trigger events
- 5 affiliate conversions
- 3 firm/affiliate partnership conversations

## 12-month vision

- 200+ firm profiles
- 10,000+ registered traders
- Verified payout proof library
- Live rule-change monitoring workflow
- Provider-backed spread data
- Paid Pro subscription base
- Business/API tier
- Weekly prop firm intelligence report

## Risks

- Data accuracy and freshness
- Affiliate bias perception
- Legal/regulatory disclaimers
- Provider API costs
- Prop firm policy volatility
- Review spam/fake payout proof

## Risk mitigations

- Clearly label data source and confidence level
- Keep editorial scores separate from paid placements
- Use admin moderation and audit logs
- Start with indicative data, then upgrade provider-by-provider
- Publish affiliate disclosure and scoring methodology
- Allow firms to report corrections without buying placement
